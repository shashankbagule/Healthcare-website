Medicine Name: Paracetamol

Dosage: 500mg, twice daily

Precautions:

  • Do not exceed the recommended dose
  • Avoid alcohol
  • Consult doctor if pregnant

Side Effects:

  • Nausea
  • Liver damage (in high doses)
  • Dizziness

Medicine Name: Crocin

Dosage: 500mg, every 4-6 hours

Precautions:

  • Do not exceed 4g per day
  • Avoid alcohol
  • Consult doctor if pregnant

Side Effects:

  • Nausea
  • Allergic reactions
  • Stomach pain

Medicine Name: Aspirin

Dosage: 75-325mg daily

Precautions:

  • Do not take on an empty stomach
  • Avoid if you have bleeding disorders
  • Consult a doctor if pregnant

Side Effects:

  • Stomach ulcers
  • Bleeding risk
  • Heartburn

Medicine Name: Antiseptic Ointment

Usage: Apply a thin layer on affected area

Precautions:

  • Avoid contact with eyes
  • Do not apply on deep wounds
  • Discontinue if irritation occurs

Side Effects:

  • Skin irritation
  • Allergic reaction
  • Redness

Medicine Name: Antifungal Medications

Usage: As prescribed by doctor

Precautions:

  • Do not stop treatment early
  • Avoid in case of liver issues
  • Consult doctor if symptoms persist

Side Effects:

  • Skin rash
  • Itching
  • Digestive discomfort

Medicine Name: Anti-sickness Medications

Usage: As prescribed, usually before travel or meals

Precautions:

  • May cause drowsiness
  • Avoid alcohol
  • Consult doctor if pregnant

Side Effects:

  • Drowsiness
  • Dry mouth
  • Dizziness
